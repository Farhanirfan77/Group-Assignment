Established Since 2022

Pelangi Exclusive was founded by a group of five friends who shared a common love for cultural fashion. Together, they set out to bring a unique blend of tradition and modern style to the forefront, curating a collection of Baju Melayu and Baju Kurung that resonates with both elegance and contemporary flair.

Driven by a commitment to celebrate diversity and heritage, Pelangi Exclusive continues to thrive as a collaborative effort, offering a vibrant tapestry of traditional attire for those who appreciate the beauty of cultural expression.
